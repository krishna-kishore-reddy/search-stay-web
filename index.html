Privacy Policy
Your data, handled responsibly.
Last updated: July 2024

Search Stay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Search Stay mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: full name, email address, and mobile number.
  • Account Data: login credentials stored securely via Supabase Auth.
  • Booking Information: PG preferences, booking dates, sharing type, and meal preference.
  • Device Information: device type, operating system version, and FCM token for push notifications.
  • Usage Data: pages visited, search queries, and in-app interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your Search Stay account.
  • Process and confirm booking requests with PG providers.
  • Send you push notifications about bookings, visit requests, and account activity.
  • Improve the app's features, performance, and user experience.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations under Indian laws.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We may share your information with:

  • PG Providers: your name and contact details are shared when you make a booking or visit request.
  • Razorpay: for secure payment processing. Razorpay's privacy policy governs payment data handled by them.
  • Supabase: our backend database provider that stores app data securely.
  • Firebase (Google): for push notification delivery through FCM.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with row-level security (RLS) enabled.
  • Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send notifications about:

  • Booking request updates, including confirmations and rejections.
  • Payment requests from PG providers.
  • Visit request updates.

You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, which may affect your ability to use the platform.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@searchstay.in.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Search Stay is a mobile application and does not use browser cookies. We may use anonymous analytics tools to understand app usage patterns. No personally identifiable data is shared with analytics providers.

8. Children's Privacy

Search Stay is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party services, such as Google Maps for navigation. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external services, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you via the app or email. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@searchstay.in.

Refund Policy
Clear expectations for cancellations and refunds.

This Refund Policy explains how Search Stay handles refund requests for bookings, visit-related payments, and any other charges processed through the platform.

1. Booking Payments

Any payment made to confirm a booking, reserve a bed, or block a room is subject to the cancellation and refund rules shared at the time of booking. Some bookings may be non-refundable once the provider confirms the reservation.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be considered when a payment is charged incorrectly, a confirmed booking cannot be honored by the PG provider, or a duplicate transaction occurs. Refund approval depends on transaction status, provider confirmation, and applicable payment partner rules.

3. Cancellation by User

If you cancel a booking or service request, any refund will depend on the provider's cancellation policy, the timing of the cancellation, and whether any amount has already been committed to the property or service partner.

4. Cancellation by Provider

If a PG provider cancels a confirmed booking or is unable to provide the promised accommodation, Search Stay will work with the provider and payment partner to initiate an eligible refund or assist with an alternate resolution where possible.

5. Processing Timelines

Approved refunds are typically initiated to the original payment method. After initiation, the final credit timeline depends on your bank, card issuer, UPI app, or payment partner and may take several business days.

6. Non-Refundable Charges

Convenience fees, platform fees, or partner charges may be non-refundable where clearly disclosed during checkout or where already consumed by payment processing and related services.

7. Failed or Pending Transactions

If a payment fails but an amount appears deducted, it is usually reversed automatically by the payment provider or bank. If the reversal does not happen within the expected timeline, please contact support with the transaction details.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or raise a payment concern, email support@searchstay.in or call 7013792113 with your registered mobile number, booking details, and transaction reference.

9. Right to Review Claims

Search Stay reserves the right to review refund requests, request supporting information, and decline claims that are fraudulent, abusive, or inconsistent with the applicable booking and payment terms.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after an update means you accept the revised policy.
